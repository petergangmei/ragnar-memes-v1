Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Last updated: December 2024

üîí Privacy-First Approach

Ragnar is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data and never sell your information to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

Ragnar is a static website that operates with minimal data collection:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • No Account Creation: Our service doesn't require user registration or accounts
  • Basic Usage Data: Standard web server logs may include IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited
  • No Cookies: We don't use tracking cookies or analytics cookies

2. How We Use Information

The minimal information we may collect is used solely for:

  • Ensuring the proper functioning of our website
  • Basic security monitoring to prevent abuse
  • Understanding general usage patterns to improve the service

3. Third-Party Services

Ragnar uses the following third-party services:

  • Imgflip API: We fetch meme content from Imgflip's public API. Please review their privacy policy for more information.
  • Bootstrap CDN: We use Bootstrap's CDN for styling, which may log basic request information.

We have no control over these third-party services and encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

4. Data Storage and Security

Since Ragnar is a static website:

  • We don't store personal data in databases
  • No user-generated content is stored on our servers
  • All meme content is fetched in real-time from external APIs
  • Standard web security practices are implemented

5. Children's Privacy

Our service is family-friendly and suitable for all ages. Since we don't collect personal information, there are no special considerations for children's data. However, we encourage parental guidance for internet usage.

6. Your Rights

Since we collect minimal to no personal data, most data protection requests don't apply. However, you have the right to:

  • Stop using our service at any time
  • Clear your browser data if you have concerns about local storage
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions

7. International Users

Ragnar is accessible worldwide. Since we don't collect personal data, international data transfer regulations generally don't apply to our service.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through our website.

Questions About Privacy?

We're committed to transparency about our privacy practices. If you have any concerns or questions, please don't hesitate to reach out.
